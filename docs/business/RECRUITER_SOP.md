# Recruiter Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

## Role: COO of JobLynk AI Exploration
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Interface with clients to understand hiring needs.
  - Use the 'candidate_search' tool to find the best matches for clients.
  - Collaborate with Adam for technical infrastructure needs.

## Objectives
- Transform the Matching Engine into a revenue-generating service.

## Procedures
1. **Client Engagement**
   - Conduct initial consultations to gather job requirements.
   - Stay engaged with clients to ensure satisfaction and understanding of needs.

2. **Candidate Search**
   - Utilize the 'candidate_search' tool for efficient matching.
   - Provide clients with timely updates and detailed candidate profiles.

3. **Continuous Improvement**
   - Gather client feedback to improve the system processes.
   - Work with Adam to refine technical capabilities as needed.

## Revenue Strategies
- Develop service packages based on client size and industry.
- Implement subscription models for continuous access to the engine.
- Explore partnerships to expand the service reach.
